About

Bookify is a web platform developed as a subject project by three students from the School of Digital Communication and Multimedia Engineering, Hanoi University of Science and Technology. We created Bookify with a shared belief that reading should be easier to access, more engaging, and deeply connected to a community. Our goal is to help readers not only discover meaningful books but also share their thoughts and experiences with others who share the same passion.
